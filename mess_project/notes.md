📄 Product Brief — Single-Brand Food E-commerce

1. Business Model
   Type: Single-brand (your own restaurant chain)

Fulfillment: Delivery only

Vendor signup: Open signup with Google/Facebook free APIs (social login for customers/vendors if multi-kitchen internally)

Revenue Model:

Primary: Listing fees for vendors

Optional: Subscription model for extra options & free delivery for customers

2. Menu & Food Offering
   Scope: Restaurant food + snacks + beverages

Menu updates: Daily specials + seasonal offers

Customization: Yes (toppings, spice level, combos)

Dietary filters: Veg, Jain, Vegan, Gluten-free

3. Target Audience
   Segments: College students, office workers, families

Initial Market: City-level (expand to PAN India later)

Languages: English first, regional languages later

4. Delivery & Logistics
   Delivery Team: TBD (in-house or 3rd party)

Live Tracking: Yes (map view with ETA)

Delivery SLA: Target under 30 mins in city limits

5. Payments
   Methods: UPI, Cards, Wallets (Paytm, PhonePe, etc.)

Loyalty/Wallet: Not in MVP; consider in future

6. Promotions & Marketing
   Here’s where I’ll give you a plan:

Day-One Promotions:

Launch offers: Flat ₹100 off first order above ₹300, or “Free Drink with First Meal”.

Limited-time bundles: E.g., “Family Combo” with a 15% discount.

Restaurant Discounts:

Since you’re single-brand, these will be system-wide offers (set by you, not vendors).

Can be time-bound (Lunch Hour Deals, Weekend Specials).

Subscription Plan:

Name: e.g., “FastBite Plus”

Perks:

Free delivery on orders above ₹200

Priority kitchen preparation

Exclusive menu items for members

Pricing: ₹99/month or ₹899/year

Retention Tools:

Push notifications for re-engagement

Seasonal campaigns (Diwali special menu)

Referral program (Invite friends, earn ₹50 credits)

7. Legal & Compliance
   GST invoice generation: Yes

Legal documents: Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, Refund Policy

FSSAI compliance: Yes (vendor onboarding process must verify license)

8. Branding & Design
   Brand Name: Not decided yet

Logo: Not designed yet

Look & Feel: Fun & vibrant like Zomato but with a slightly more premium touch for trust.
